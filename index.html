002 — How I work

004 — Process

How a project actually goes.

Step 00

Sharpen.

A 30-minute call. We work out whether you actually need me — or a freelancer, or a weekend of focus. Sometimes it's the third one.

Step 01

Scope.

A one-page brief. The smallest thing I can ship that proves the idea works. Fixed price, fixed date, agreed in a thread.

Step 02

Build.

Regular demos and a staging URL from day one. You see the thing exist before you sign off on the next milestone.

Step 03

Hand off.

You get the repo, the runbook, and a stretch of free patch support. We stay on retainer only if you actually want to.
